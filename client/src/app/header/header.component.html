What is the "Projected" gray graph?
The projected graph is an estimate of how busy it is going to be. Projections are made daily using the past recorded data.
*Note* That if the purple graph (today's real time data) is trending slightly below the projected line, it will probably slightly quieter than the projection suggests!

What's with the futuristic gray numbers?
These are also estimates of how busy it is going to be.
